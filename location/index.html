The Bed & Breakfast Dai Nonni is rightly located in the heart of the historical center of Verona, near the central pedestrian shopping street and only 200 meters far from the Arena. The area is also well served by public transports and you can easily reach the conferences' site at the Hotel San Marco. About the agreement: jsDay convention (70€ per night for a double bedroom, with private bathroom, including breakfast).

How to get to the conference

Route from Verona Nord Exit

Take the Tangenziale following Verona Centro direction. At the end of the Tangenziale turn left. Continue towards Porta Palio. At the roundabout go straight along Viale Colonnello Galliano. At the first traffic light turn left into Via San Marco. Drive straight along Via San Marco to the Hotel.

Route from Verona Sud Exit

Follow the direction to Fiera - Verona Centro. Drive along Viale delle Nazioni. Continue along Viale del Lavoro and Viale del Piave. Once you arrive in Piazzale Porta Nuova, turn left. Drive along Viale Luciano Dal Cero. Continue towards Porta Palio. At the roundabout go straight along Viale Colonnello Galliano. At the first traffic light turn left into Via San Marco. Drive straight along Via San Marco to the Hotel.

Route from the Airport

Take the Tangenziale following Verona direction. Take the junction to Verona Centro. At the end of the Tangenziale turn left. Continue towards Porta Palio. At the roundabout go straight along Viale Colonnello Galliano. At the first traffic light turn left into Via San Marco. Drive straight along Via San Marco to the Hotel.

Route from the Railway Station

Reaching the hotel from the railway station is quite easy, you only need to take bus number 11 or 95 at platform “C” direction “CHIEVO” (here is the time schedule for bus #11 and bus #95). The run will be from “STAZIONE PORTA NUOVA” to “VIA LONGHENA 40“, which is really near the hotel; if you miss that stop, step down at “VIALE MANZONI 21/B” that is 300m after.

You can also get to the venue by taxi. They are near the exit, on the right.
